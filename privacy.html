Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  |  D'Vaaw Perfume, Kota Samarahan, Sarawak

1. Who We Are

D'Vaaw Perfume ("we", "us", "our") is a fragrance brand based in Kota Samarahan, Sarawak, Malaysia. We sell Halal luxury lifestyle fragrances through this website. By using this site, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Cart data: Items you add to your cart are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you proceed to checkout.
  • Order data: When you place an order via WhatsApp, your name, phone number, shipping address, and order details are shared with us directly through WhatsApp.
  • Firebase: Order summaries (items, totals) are logged to our Firebase Firestore database for record-keeping. No payment card data is stored.
  • Contact form: If you submit our contact form, the message is sent to us via WhatsApp and is not stored on any server.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To improve our website and product offerings
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) to save your shopping cart between visits. This data is stored entirely on your device. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics at this time.

5. WhatsApp & Third-Party Services

Our ordering and contact system routes through WhatsApp (Meta). When you tap "Proceed to WhatsApp", your order details are pre-filled into a WhatsApp message sent to our business number. WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy applies to messages sent via their platform.

We use Firebase (Google) to log order records. Google's Privacy Policy governs data stored in Firebase.

6. Data Retention

Cart data in localStorage is cleared automatically when you complete a checkout. Order records in Firebase are retained for up to 2 years for business record purposes. WhatsApp message history is subject to your own WhatsApp retention settings.
