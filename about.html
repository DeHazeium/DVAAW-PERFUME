Our Origin

Why Gula Apong?

Gula Apong — Nypa Palm Sugar — is one of Sarawak's most treasured natural gifts. It is gentle, long-lasting, and deeply rooted in the land. These are the same qualities we wanted in every bottle of D'Vaaw.

When we chose Gula Apong as the soul of our brand, we weren't just picking an ingredient. We were making a declaration: that luxury doesn't have to come from somewhere else. It can come from here. From us. From Borneo.

Our Mission

Uplift. Empower. Inspire.

D'Vaaw was founded with a clear purpose: to create meaningful livelihoods for single mothers and students in Sarawak. Every bottle sold contributes to this mission.

We believe that when a woman puts on D'Vaaw, she's not just wearing a fragrance. She's wearing proof that community, resilience, and beauty can all exist in the same breath.
